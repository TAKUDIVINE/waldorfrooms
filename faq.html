Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a form?

Click on the appropriate Tenant or Landlord form and fill in your details.

Are listings verified?

Yes, all rooms are verified before being shared with tenants.

Do tenants pay any fee?

No, tenants do not pay any fee for using Waldorf Rooms.

How does Waldorf Rooms make money?

Landlords pay for pre-screened tenant leads and room-matching support.

Can I visit a room before deciding?

Yes. Visits are arranged through Divine once both parties are ready.

Are my contact details posted publicly?

No. Phone numbers and addresses stay private until appropriate.

Which areas are currently covered?

Waldorf, La Plata, Bryans Road, and Accokeek in Maryland.

Can landlords choose who they accept?

Yes. Divine guides tenants, but landlords always make the final decision.
